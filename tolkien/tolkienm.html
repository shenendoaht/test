MORDOR

The terrible mountain kingdom of Sauron the Dark Lord throughout the Second and Third Ages. Within this realm Sauron forges the One Ring in the volcanic fires of Mount Doom and gathers vast armies of Orcs, Trolls, Uruks, Southrons, and Easterlings, all in his mission to dominate and enslave the kingdoms of the Elves and Men of Middle-Earth.

Tolkien likely derived the name for this evil kingdom from the Anglo-Saxon mordor, meaning murder or mortal sin. This seems an appropriate name for an evil kingdom created as an engine for slaughter. However, in Tolkien's own invented languages Mordor means Black Land in the Sindarin Grey Elven tongue and Land of Shadow in the Quenya language of the Eldar.
