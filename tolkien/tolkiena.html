AEGIR

A Norse nature spirit, comparable to the Greco-Roman sea-god Pontus and Tolkien's Osse, the Maia of the Waves. Aegir was subservient to Njord, the Norse god of the sea, just as Osse is to Tolkien's Ulmo, the Lord of the Waters. Like Osse and Pontus, Aegir was known for his wildly shifting moods and his tempestuous nature, making Aegir much feared by mariners.
